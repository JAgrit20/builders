Mozilla is investing $200 million to fix the internet.

The internet’s amazing, weird, sometimes a little icky. Let’s face it: it could use some fixing.

Mozilla has set aside $200 million over the next 3 years to support coders, entrepreneurs and creators in areas including: (1) collaboration, esp. urgent in this global moment of crisis, (2) privacy and security, (3) alternatives to surveillance capitalism, (4) polarization & misinformation, (5) AI that works for communities and citizens, and (6) the next generation of the web, decentralized.

People like you joined together to build Firefox 15 years ago. People like you will write the next amazing chapter of the internet.
